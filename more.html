About Company

In 2019 Project Phoenix was founded in Enactus UP its sole purpose was to introduce informal or small brick makers in townships to the use of ash as a supplement to manufacturing bricks. This mutually beneficial relationship allowed brick makers to reduce the cost of brick manufacturing and present the opportunity for companies to dispose of their waste (ash) in an environmentally friendly way.

After two years of working closely with brick makers in townships in Pretoria, South Africa our co- founders Londiwe Mvula (COO) and Lebogang Seakatsie (CEO) soon realised that although reducing the cost of manufacturing bricks was beneficial it was just not enough. With a burning passion for the development of small businesses and the recognised need for environmental sustainability, Sakhisizwe and Associates Social Enterprise was born. We are creating the sustainable development of an environmentally conscious community of small township businesses through the introduction of non-fried bricks made of upcycled ash. Our goal is to achieve the increased usage of green building materials in South Africa through the intentional development of small township businesses.
