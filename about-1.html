OUR PHILOSOPHY

We believe that there is a better and innovative solution that combines efficient waste management. Innovative structural development and energy effeciency all in one. Like Thomas Edison said, ``There's a better way to do it, find it``.

We found it! We are using the waste of today to build a better tomorrow. Through our ash bricks we are able to solve the problem of ash disposal in landfills and break the cycle of poverty in communities in a sustainable way all at the same time.

Waste Management

Your waste is someones treasure! Sakhisizwe waste management prides itself in disposing our clients waste in a safe, cost effective and efficient manner. The waste is distributed to our partners who upcycle it and turn it around into bricks.
