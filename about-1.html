VISION

Sakhisizwe and Associates Social Enterprise seeks to pioneer the rebirth of South African communities business by business through the redefinition of ash waste across South African by upcycling ash that enables the innovative pro-creation of green building materials. .

MISSION

Pioneers empowering the community to build their community, while leading in green building material innovation in an ever dynamic globe.

OBJECTIVES

  1. Reducing the amount of ash disposal in landfills.
  2. Producing quality ash building materials.
  3. Developing sustainable businesses in the townships we are working in.
