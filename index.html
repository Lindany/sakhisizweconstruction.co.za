About Company

In 2019 Project Phoenix was founded in Enactus UP its sole purpose was to introduce informal or small brick makers in townships to the use of ash as a supplement to manufacturing bricks. This would mutually benefit brick makers and landfill sites as brick makers would reduce the cost of manufacturing bricks whilst also reducing the amount of ash being dumped in landfills.After two years of working closely with brick makers in townships in Pretoria, South Africa our co- founders Ms Londiwe Mvula (COO) and Ms Lebogang Seakatsie (CEO) soon realised that although reducing the cost of manufacturing bricks was beneficial it was just not enough. With a burning passion for the development of small businesses and the recognised need for environmental sustainability, Sakhisizwe and Associates Social Enterprise was born. The main purpose of our company is to create a sustainable development of an environmentally conscious community of small businesses through the introduction of non-fried bricks made of upcycled ash. We aim to provide access to market for the small businesses we work with as well as business development training such as accounting fundamentals, marketing fundamentals, and brick-making training to name a few. As a business, we aim to achieve the increased usage of green building materials in South Africa through the intentional development of small businesses.
